Understanding Our Opinion Metric

How we analyze and measure media bias in news articles

How It Works

1

Article Collection

We continuously gather news articles from various sources across the web.

2

AI Analysis

Our advanced AI models process each article analyzing semantic patterns to identify bias.

3

Scoring

Articles receive an opinion score based on multiple factors, including language, sources, and context.

Understanding the Scores

1-2: Highly Opinionated

Strong bias indicators, heavy use of subjective language

3-4: Moderately Opinionated

Mix of factual reporting with notable opinion elements

5: Fact-Focused

Primarily factual reporting with minimal opinion content
